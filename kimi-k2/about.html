My Journey into AI

From curious student to AI educator - a path of continuous learning and sharing

2014

The Beginning

Started my PhD in Computer Science at MIT, focusing on machine learning algorithms. This is where my fascination with artificial intelligence truly began. I spent countless nights researching neural networks and their applications in real-world problems.

2017

Industry Breakthrough

Joined Google AI as a Research Scientist, working on computer vision projects. Led a team that developed innovative image recognition algorithms that improved accuracy by 15% over existing methods. This experience taught me the power of collaborative AI development.

2020

Teaching Discovery

Began teaching AI courses at Stanford as an adjunct professor. Discovered my passion for education when I saw students have their "aha" moments. The joy of watching someone understand a complex concept for the first time was incredibly rewarding.

2022

Coaching Evolution

Launched my AI coaching practice after realizing that professionals needed personalized guidance beyond traditional classroom learning. Developed methodologies that combine theoretical knowledge with practical, hands-on experience.

2024

Global Impact

Today, I've trained over 500 professionals across 15 countries, helping them transition into AI roles, advance their careers, and lead AI initiatives in their organizations. Every success story motivates me to reach more people.

My Teaching Philosophy

Education is not about transferring information – it's about transformation. I believe in empowering professionals with both the knowledge and confidence to lead in the AI era.

Personalized Learning

Every professional has unique goals, backgrounds, and learning styles. I customize each training program to match individual needs and career objectives.

Hands-On Practice

Theory without practice is just information. Every concept is reinforced with real-world projects and case studies that build practical skills and confidence.

Learning is most effective when immediately applied. I help students implement AI solutions in their current roles, creating immediate value for their organizations.

AI evolves rapidly. I teach students how to stay current with emerging trends and technologies, creating a foundation for lifelong learning in the field.

Collaborative Growth
